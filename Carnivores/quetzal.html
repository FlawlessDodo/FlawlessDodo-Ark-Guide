Quetzal

Creature of the Month!!

The Quetzal is the only non raid flyer with a platform saddle, making it an extremely useful asset to have. Its uses are near infinite with the platform saddle. It can be used for transport, storage, being a base itself, and much more. There is so much I can say good about it, but not much bad. It may be rare or hard to tame, but is well worth it in the end!
