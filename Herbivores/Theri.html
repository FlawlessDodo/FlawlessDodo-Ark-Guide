Therizino

The Therizino, one of the best combat creatures in the game. It is easily stronger than a Spino, with the ability to almost beat a Rex in head to head combat! It has good attack speed, low stamina consumption, good sprinting speed, and since they are Herbivores, can consume the healing veggie cakes.
